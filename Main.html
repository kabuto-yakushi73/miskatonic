Why Miskatonic

A tradition of excellence, a culture of inquiry, a commitment to the world.

Rigorous Academics

With over 40 departments and professional schools, our curriculum spans the arts, sciences, and professions, preparing students for leadership in every field.

World-Class Research

From the Arkham National Laboratory to the Antarctic, our researchers push the boundaries of knowledge in fields critical to humanity's future.

Historic Campus

Our main campus in Arkham blends centuries-old Gothic architecture with state-of-the-art facilities, creating an inspiring environment for learning and discovery.

Global Community

With students and faculty from over 100 countries and partnerships with leading institutions worldwide, Miskatonic is truly a global university.
